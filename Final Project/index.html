Key Information about Gas equipment

  • Gas powered lawn equipment uses an infeccient, unregulated two stroke engine, resulting in significant pollution
  • Running a leaf blower for one hour is the equivalent emissions of driving 1,000 miles in a gas car
  • Noise levels can reach levels dangerous to human health
